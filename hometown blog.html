About Mananthavady

Mananthavady is a picturesque town located in the Wayanad district of Kerala, India.

Nestled in the Western Ghats, it's known for its lush green landscapes, rich tribal culture, and pleasant climate.

Key Features

  • Elevation: Approximately 800 meters above sea level
  • Famous for coffee, tea and spice plantations
  • Home to various indigenous tribal communities
  • Gateway to several wildlife sanctuaries
